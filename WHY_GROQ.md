# 🌍 Why We Use Groq (Not Gemini)

## The Problem with Gemini
Google's Gemini AI is **not available in many countries** including:
- Indonesia
- Most of Africa
- Many countries in Asia
- Parts of South America
- And others

This makes it unsuitable for a truly global bot.

## Why Groq is Better

### ✅ Available Worldwide
- Works in **ALL countries**
- No geographic restrictions
- No VPN needed

### ✅ Higher Free Limits
- **14,400 requests/day** (vs Gemini's 1,500)
- That's **9.6x more requests**!
- Perfect for daily prompts + file uploads

### ✅ Faster Performance
- Groq specializes in **ultra-fast AI inference**
- Questions generate in **under 1 second**
- Better user experience

### ✅ Same Quality
- Uses **Llama 3.1 70B** model
- Comparable to GPT-4 and Gemini Pro
- Excellent for educational questions

## Technical Comparison

| Feature | Groq | Gemini |
|---------|------|--------|
| **Availability** | 🌍 Worldwide | ⚠️ Limited countries |
| **Free Requests/Day** | 14,400 | 1,500 |
| **Speed** | Ultra-fast | Fast |
| **Model** | Llama 3.1 70B | Gemini Pro |
| **Quality** | Excellent | Excellent |
| **Setup** | Easy | Easy (where available) |
